About the project

Watch Commercial

A TV commercial for a fictional watch brand emiT. The Ad showcases the product and it's highlighting features. I created the model and textured it in Blender. Then I animated the watch and the camera to capture various shots for the commercial. Then I added lights and rendered the scene using Cycles. After rendering the shots in an image sequence, I imported them into DaVinci Resolve for the final edit. I added titles, sound effects and video filters to achieve the final look for the commercial.

Software used - Blender, DaVinci Resolve
